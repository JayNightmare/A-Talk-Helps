About Talking Helps

Talking Helps is an anonymous space where you can share your thoughts and feelings without fear of judgment. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive environment for open discussion about mental health and emotional well-being.

Our Principles

  • Anonymity: We never collect personal information or track your identity.
  • Support: Our community is built on mutual respect and support.
  • Accessibility: Free and open to anyone who needs a space to talk.

Privacy & Safety

We take your privacy seriously. All posts and comments are anonymous, and we use automated systems to maintain a safe environment. If you see something concerning, please use the report feature or contact us directly.
